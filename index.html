The Problem

Why Agents Lose Members
at Every AEP

The Medicare market has a fundamental member retention problem — driven by agent silence, market disruption, and unused benefits. All four are addressable with the right year-round engagement strategy.

Annual Enrollment Period Is Too Late
By the time AEP arrives, dissatisfied members have already decided to switch. Waiting until October to re-engage is fighting an uphill battle every year.
Agents Can't Scale High-Touch Service
A licensed agent managing hundreds of clients cannot provide personalized, proactive service to every member. Most feel like a policy number — not a person with a trusted advisor.
Unused Benefits Create Dissatisfaction
Members who never use dental, vision, OTC, or wellness benefits blame their agent and carrier — even when the coverage was available all along.
Market Forces Are Forcing Members to Shop
Medicare Advantage plan switching rates have doubled since 2023 due to plan terminations, ANOC changes, and network disruptions — forcing many satisfied members to re-shop anyway. Agents with strong year-round relationships are far more likely to retain these members through the disruption.

At-Risk Intelligence

Identify, Prioritize & Reduce
At-Risk Member Scores

Retention-GPT automatically surfaces at-risk members using three signals: negative or declining sentiment, low or zero engagement, and plan-level risk factors including anticipated plan terminations and major ANOC changes.

As the agency team engages with at-risk members — answering questions, resolving concerns, and activating benefits — the system tracks those interactions and automatically reduces each member's risk score over time. The dashboard continuously reprioritizes the list so the team always knows who needs attention most.

Plan Termination Alerts
BookCover monitors CMS plan data to flag members enrolled in plans at risk of termination, giving agents a head start before AEP
ANOC Change Tracking
Significant Annual Notice of Change items are detected and flagged — members on affected plans are prioritized for proactive outreach
Churn Prediction Score

How It Works

Your Agent Gets the Credit.
